# Telegram Music Bot  

A fully advanced Telegram Music Bot with AI capabilities, voice command support, story mode, and high-quality music streaming.  

## Features  
- **Music Streaming**: High-quality music playback in voice chats.  
- **AI Chat Assistant**: Intelligent responses and discussions.  
- **Playlist System**: Custom playlists with queue management.  
- **Voice Filters & Effects**: Various sound effects and filters.  
- **Lullaby & Story Mode**: Narrates lullabies and stories based on user mood.  
- **Auto-Restart**: Ensures continuous operation.  
- **Admin Controls**: Full moderation and management tools.
